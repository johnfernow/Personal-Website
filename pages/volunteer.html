Volunteer Work

I have hosted a blood drive for the American Red Cross in Lancaster, Ohio and typically donate blood every two months, depending on my hemoglobin levels. So far I've donated over 2 gallons (3.79 liters)!

Citizens Climate Lobby is a nonprofit, nonpartisan, international, grassroots organization that strives to introduce environmental legislation widely-backed by climate scientists and economists alike. I have volunteered to meet with Congressional aides, give speeches, and recruit new members to the organization.
