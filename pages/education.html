Denison University
Undergrad

I will graduate in May 2020 with a bachelors in Computer Science. According to the 2020 U.S. News & World Report college and university rankings, Denison ranked 45th in "Best Undergraduate Teaching" in the US. A compilation of reviews can be viewed here.

At Denison, I took the following classes:

  • Data Structures
  • Data Systems
  • Computer Systems
  • Algorithm Design and Analysis
  • Game Design
  • Mathematical Foundations of Computer Science
  • Intermediate Computer Science
  • Introduction to Computer Science: Scientific Data
